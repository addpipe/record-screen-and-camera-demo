Recording Your Screen And Camera Demo

Made by the Pipe Recording Platform

This demo uses getDisplayMedia(), MediaStream Recording API and HTML Canvas to record the screen and your camera at the same time.

Works on:

  • Chrome 72 and up
  • Edge 79 and up
  • Firefox 66 and up
  • Opera 60 and up
